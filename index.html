What I offer

Coaching for every part of your game

Build a reliable, powerful swing from the ground up. Using Trackman data and high-speed video, Brian identifies the root cause of inconsistency and gives you a clear, structured path to fixing it permanently.

More shots are dropped within 50 yards than anywhere else on the course. Brian’s short game system covers chipping, pitching, flop shots and bunker play — giving you the confidence to get up and down from anywhere.

The flat stick is where scorecards are made or ruined. Develop a consistent stroke, improve your green reading and learn the pace control techniques used by tour professionals to convert more birdie opportunities.
